Tips for Working out at Home

Going out for a Walk/Run

Getting out for a Walk or a Run will help allow you to get some exercise but also get you out of the house. As long as during this time that you keep proper social distancing, move off the sidewalk/trail if you are able. There are some places that are under lockdown where this is not allowed. Make sure you can be outside before going.

Planks

Planks are a great core exercise that is easy to do and requires no equipment. There are many different forms that you can use based on skill level. Check out this link for indepth details.

HIIT

HIIT stands for High Intensity Interval Training. You can do most HIIT classes just with yourself. No equipment required. Here is a Link to a great Youtube channel Fitness Blender.

Yoga

Yoga is a great way to stretch out sore muscles, increase flexibility, and releive stress. here is a link for a 20 min yoga class on Youtube. Namaste!

By working out you can increase your energy, boost your immune system, the release of feel good hormones -like dopamine and serotonin- and increases self esteem and reducess the risk of depression. 20 mins is best but don't be down on yourself if you get only 7 mins. Any exercise helps and is beneficial.
