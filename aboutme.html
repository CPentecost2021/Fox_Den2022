About Christopher The Professional

Software Quality Assurance Analyst

I started in QA 6 years ago. I came into the position asa subject matter expert. After working for 13 years at the same company you get to know the software you are working with. From there I learned on the job day one.

The Important Skills and Knowledge I have gained: Agile Methodologies, Software Development Life Cycle, White and Black Box Testing, Functional, Load, Exploratory, End to End, Regression, Mobile, Smoke and Integration Testing.

I have also worked with API testing with Swagger and the automated testing suite ReadyAPI. As well as Project Management software Jira.

About Christopher The Person

Outdoor Activities

Working Out

I enjoy working out. Both cardiovascular and weight training. I try to go 2-3 times a week on my own and at least 1 day a week with my trainer Lisle.

I do this mainly to keep myself in good condition to keep up with my kids, keep myself healthy and try to reverse some health issues.

I am a firm believer you get out of it what you put in...so I am never not putting in the work.
